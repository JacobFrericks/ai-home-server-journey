**Date:** December 5, 2024

**Tags:** homelab, ai, setup

Welcome to my blog documenting the adventure of building an AI-powered home server!

I've decided to take the plunge into self-hosting AI models and services at home, and I want to share everything I learn along the way.

## Why a Home Server for AI?

With the rise of powerful open-source AI models like Llama, Mistral, and Stable Diffusion, it's now possible to run impressive AI capabilities on consumer hardware. Here's why I'm excited about this journey:

- **Privacy**: Keep your data local and under your control
- **Cost**: Avoid ongoing API costs for AI services
- **Learning**: Deep dive into AI infrastructure and deployment
- **Customization**: Fine-tune and customize models for personal use

## What to Expect

In this blog, I'll be covering:

1. **Hardware Selection**: Choosing the right components for AI workloads
2. **Software Stack**: Setting up the operating system, containers, and AI frameworks
3. **Model Deployment**: Running local LLMs, image generation, and more
4. **Self-Hosted Services**: Integrating AI with other home services

Stay tuned for more posts as I build out this setup. I'll share both my successes and the inevitable challenges along the way!
